About

A Small Town Guy is an American comedy/drama created by Chima Oparaji, that chronicles his life growing up in the small town of Tuxedo Park New York, and eventually attending Princeton University. It premiered on September 20th, 2019 and is currently on its 5th season. Each season revolves around a different year in Chima’s life, which directly correlates with an older age and higher grade, but often indirectly correlates with Chima making new friends, losing old friends, and other types of relationship progressions or digressions. Through all these changes though, Chima progressively grows as a person, learning how to conduct himself better in various situations and how to be a better friend when needed.

Characters

The characters in this series can be divided into two: those before Chima goes to Prineton, and those he meets at Princeton. Something special about this show is the way it foucses on other characters outside of Chima, with the other characters often having their own episodes of focus, inner monologues, etc.

Series Overview

Season 1

Airdates: September 20th, 2019 - May 29th, 2020

Season 1 takes place during Chima's Freshmen year of High School, before Covid 19 lockdown

Season 2

Airdates: August 28th, 2020 - September 10th, 2021

Season 2 takes place during Chima's sophmore year of highschool, where he learned remotely for most of the year, and went back in person towards the end.

Season 3

Airdates: September 24th, 2021 - July 8th, 2022

Season 3 takes place during Chima's junior year of High School, where everything is back to normal for the most part, however a lot fo things have shifted socially due to a lot of people leaving the school.

Season 4

Airdates: August 5th, 2022 - July 14th, 2023

Season 4 takes place during Chima's senior year of High School, where Chima is applying to colleges, and eventually gets into Princeton University. As this happens, Chima also thinks about the mark he wants to leave on his school district.

Season 5

Airdates: August 4th, 2023 - May 31st, 2024

Season 5 takes place during Chima's first year of college. Even though Princeton was a smaller school compared to a lot of other colleges, Chima coming from a very small town was initially intimidated by the grandier of everything, but overtime, learned to get adjusted to the community and academics.
